Werpsyn is independently developed. We're grateful for any support from our users.

Supporting the Project

Supporting Werpsyn development is optional. Your contributions help us:

  • Maintain and improve the app
  • Add new features and functionality
  • Keep the app running and secure
  • Provide ongoing support
Important: Contributions are optional and do not unlock app features, digital content, subscriptions, premium access or special access inside the iOS app. Werpsyn remains fully functional regardless of whether you choose to contribute.

How to Contribute

If you'd like to support the development of Werpsyn, contribution options will be available here in the future.

Questions?

If you have questions about supporting the project, please contact us at info@ontwerps.com.
