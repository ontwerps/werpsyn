Support

Get help with Werpsyn

We're here to help. If you're experiencing issues with Werpsyn or have questions, please reach out to us.

Contact Support

For support inquiries, please email us at:

Email: info@ontwerps.com

When reporting an issue, please include the following information to help us assist you faster:

  • Device Model: (e.g., iPhone 15, iPad Air)
  • iOS Version: (e.g., iOS 17.4)
  • App Version: (found in App Store or Settings → General → About → Werpsyn)
  • Issue Description: A clear description of the problem you're experiencing

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a problem?

Email us at info@ontwerps.com with a detailed description of the issue. Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and steps to reproduce the problem if possible.

Where can I find the privacy policy?

The privacy policy for Werpsyn is available here. It outlines how we collect and use your data.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 48 hours during business days.
