Privacy Policy

Werpsyn iOS App

Effective Date: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Werpsyn iOS app developed by Ontwerps ("we," "us," "our").

1. App

Werpsyn is an iOS application available on the Apple App Store. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide app functionality. This may include:

  • Device information (iOS version, device model, app version)
  • Usage information about app features you interact with

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Health or sensitive personal information without explicit consent
  • Contact information without your consent

3. Analytics and Diagnostics

We may use crash reporting and analytics to improve app stability and performance. This helps us identify and fix issues affecting the app experience. No personal information is included in diagnostic reports.

4. Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your personal data for third-party advertising or marketing purposes.

Any personal data you provide is used only to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the app experience

5. Backend Services

Some app features may communicate with backend services operated by Ontwerps to provide functionality such as data synchronization or feature services. These backend services are subject to the same privacy principles outlined in this policy.
